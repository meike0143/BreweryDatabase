About the brewery types

There are many different types of breweries, each with a different meaning:

  • Micro:Most craft breweries.
  • Nano:An extremely smlal brewery which typically only distributes locally.
  • Regional:A regional location of an expanded brewery.
  • Brewpub:A beer-focussed restaurant or restaurant/bar with a brewery on premise.
  • Large:A very large brewery, likely not for visitors.
  • Planning:A brewery in planning or not yet opened to the public.
  • Bar:A bar with no brewery equipment on premise.
  • Contract:A brewery that uses another brewery's equipment
  • Proprietor:Similar to contact brewing but refers more to a brewery incubator.
  • Closed:A location which has been closed.
